Self Employed Cleaner Job Application

Job Description

We are looking for dedicated and reliable self employed cleaners to join our team. As a cleaner, you will be responsible for providing top-notch cleaning services to our clients. Your duties will include sweeping, mopping, dusting, vacuuming, and sanitising various surfaces.

Responsibilities:

  • Clean and sanitise rooms, bathrooms, kitchens, and other areas as assigned.
  • Ensure all cleaning tasks are completed to the highest standards.
  • Report any maintenance issues or damages found during cleaning.
  • Follow health and safety regulations at all times.

Requirements:

  • Proven experience as a cleaner or housekeeper.
  • Ability to work independently and manage time effectively.
  • Attention to detail and a commitment to high-quality work.
  • Flexibility to work in different locations as required.
  • Must have own Insurance or willing to get one
  • Ability to drive is Preferred but not neccessary

If you are passionate about providing excellent cleaning services and enjoy working independently, we encourage you to apply today!

This application form is intended to collect all necessary information from candidates applying for the cleaner position at Shine Bright Cleaning. Please ensure all sections are completed accurately. Incomplete applications may not be considered. Please submit the completed application form along with your resume/CV and any relevant certificates. Failure to send these will delay your application.
